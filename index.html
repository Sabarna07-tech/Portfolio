Early Detection of Alzheimer's Disease Using Deep Learning and SMOTE for Class Imbalance Correction

Jotiraditya Banerjee, Sabarna Saha, Piyush Gupta, Abhijit Chandra

menu_book International Conference on Frontiers in Computing and Systems (COMSYS 2024), Volume 1, pp. 469-481. Springer Nature Singapore

Quick prevention and efficient treatment for Alzheimer's disease (AD) rely upon early diagnosis. This study proposes a unique method that combines deep learning strategies with the Synthetic Minority Oversampling Technique (SMOTE) to overcome the inherent class imbalance in an AD dataset. Our proposed convolutional neural network (CNN) model is designed to accurately detect AD in its early stages, thus offering significant potential to enhance diagnostic outcomes. Through the integration of SMOTE, we ensure a balanced representation of all classes, improving the model's robustness.
